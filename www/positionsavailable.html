Cornell Cinema is accepting applications from Cornell students for the following positions. Applicants should drop off an application at the Cornell Cinema Office, 104 Willard Straight Hall. Applicants for the Display Case Coordinator position should also include a cover letter explaining their interest and qualifications. 

Office Assistant
Cornell Cinema Office Assistants help with the daily and monthly operations of the office and promotion of films. Responsibilities include filing, data entry, answering phones, and mailing in a fun, artistic and busy atmosphere. Commitment is 8 hours per week.

Volunteer Usher
Work one night a week ripping tickets and watching films, and help make Cornell Cinema the best theatre on earth!

All positions receive a free pass for two to all regular Cornell Cinema screenings.
