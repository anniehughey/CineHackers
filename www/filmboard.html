Cornell Cinema is seeking applicants for a new Cornell Cinema Student Film Board

(first meeting will be held in Fall 2006)

Activities:

  • Attend seven meetings annually with the Cornell Cinema Director (meetings will run about 90 minutes)
  • Participate in programming discussions and decisions
  • Program specialty nights at Cornell Cinema
  • Create, organize and host an annual fundraising event
  • Attend special private screenings of upcoming movies
  • Collect formal and informal input from your "constituents"
  • Help represent Cornell Cinema at any relevant Student Assembly meetings, particularly when the Activities Fee is being reviewed
  • Create your own personal email network to promote Cornell Cinema events
  • Participate in grassroots advertising and promotion

Benefits:

  • Receive complimentary guest passes to Cornell Cinema screenings
  • Get invited to Cinema Socials
  • Receive advance passes to Sneak Previews
  • Receive discounts to special events
  • Receive discounts at the Concession Stand
  • Receive a Cornell Cinema t-shirt
