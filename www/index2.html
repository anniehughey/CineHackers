This Week's Offerings

Monday is your last chance to see The Great Dictator, Charlie Chaplin's satire/protest of Hitler. Errol Morris's unsettling and controversial documentary about Abu Ghraib, Standard Operating Procedure, has its first screening this Wednesday. Music Docs this week include: Glass: A Portrait of Philip in Twelve Parts—an intimate look at the minimalist composer during a busy year of composition and family turmoil, it is “as haunting in its own way as one of Glass’s own works.” (New York Magazine) and a fascinating look at the year-long hand-crafting that goes into a single Steinway piano in Note by Note: The Making of Steinway L1307

For your comedy fix, we offer Harold and Kumar Escape from Guantanamo Bay; for those in need of an action fix, how about both Jet Li and Jackie Chan in The Forbidden Kingdom?; and finally tragic period romance is at its finest in The Duchess of Langeais.
