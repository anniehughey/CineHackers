  • ’70s Cinema

    Altman • Coppola • Polanski • Scorsese • DeNiro • Dunaway • Hackman • Nicholson • nuff said?

    The Russian visionary’s latest - his take on Faust - and his one-take tour of the Hermitage Museum, Russian Ark

    Two free events: Grass, a 1925 silent with live accompaniment; & Bill Morrison’s Miners’ Hymns with acclaimed soundtrack

    Cinematic explorations of worlds micro & macro, put in context by local experts. Plus a filmmaker visit!

    Four Ithaca premieres plus Blue is the Warmest Color, an erotic coming of age tale garnering multiple awards

    A Cornell alumna acts in 12 Years A Slave; another protests SeaWorld; a third visits Brooklyn with a composer.

    Riot Grrl! power • Wiseman's Berkeley • Gondr's Chomsky • contemporary art in China • claymation & Cambodia • & more

    Cult classics of unreliable memories, plus director Paul Verhoeven presenting a restoration of Total Recall

  • It’s Oscar Season!

    Get the skinny on all the Oscar nominated shorts! You can master the betting pool!

    Insects up close, fabulous cats, food-animal hybrids, international shorts, & Disney’s ‘cool’ latest!

    Claude Lanzmann's (Shoah) latest, plus three Sunday morning screenings with bagels and coffee.

    Some of 2014’s best, plus a Q&A with Austin Bunn, co-writer of Kill Your Darlings!

    Six of Hitchock’s nine surviving British silents, all restored, five featuring live accompaniment!

    More from the great Japanese animé studio! Shape-shifting raccoons, a WW1 ace (& pig), and adventures with a cat
