Secretary

Jared Landsman
Hello, my name is Jared Landsman and I am running for Secretary of the Cornell Cinema Advisory Board.  Currently, I am a freshman in the school of engineering.  I have had a passion for film all my life and my favorite childhood memories include going to see movies on the big screen.  I was actually exposed to the world of film and television at a very early age due to my father’s profession in the industry.
 
I believe I am the perfect candidate for Secretary of the Advisory Board.  In addition to my experience with film during my childhood, I am extremely organized and keep everything in an orderly manner, whether it is my daily work schedule or plans for a special event.  I am very familiar with managing and leading large groups of people.  For example, I directed a weeklong summer camp as well as a highschool chapter of Engineers without Borders.  Additionally, I have become adept at social networking and maintaining good communication between colleagues and friends.
               
Thank you very much and please vote for me, Jared Landsman, for your Secretary.

James Rowe
Hello everyone!  My name is James Rowe.  I am a Junior Economics major, and I am running for the Cornell Cinema Advisory Board Secretary.  While I do not have much experience with the Cornell Cinema Advisory Board, having only joined this semester, I have really enjoyed what the Cinema has to offer, and what the Advisory Board does to ensure that the Cornell Community has a good way to relax and enjoy cinema on a regular basis.  As Secretary, I would put in the time and effort to ensure that the Cornell Cinema remains successful, and hopefully make it even more successful in the future.  I am a very organized person, and I believe that as Secretary, I would best be able to communicate notes and decisions made by the Advisory Board to our members, as well as assisting with the organization of projects and any communication that needs to be done to ensure their success.  I ask you for your vote, and I hope that we can make next year even more successful for the Cornell Cinema than this year!
