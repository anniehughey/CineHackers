film series: fall 2015

  • Orientation Week

    Free films for new CU Students! Welcome Weekend free films all CU students! Poster Sale!

  • Orson Welles:
    A Centenary Celebration

    Eight of his finest (including Citizen Kane and Touch of Evil), a great acting turn in The Third Man, and a recent doc.

  • A Tribute to
    Don Fredericksen

    Don passed away on May 15. We will hear from colleagues and friends, screen Bergman’s Persona and follow with a reception. (free)

  • Milestone Films:
    A 25th Anniversary Tribute

    These 7 restorations include 2 works by Shirley Clarke, 2 works by Black filmmakers, and more!

  • Voices and Visions
    in Black Cinema

    Classics of African-American cinema from 1919 to 2015 introduced by Asst. Prof. Samantha Sheppard (PMA).

  • India: Then and Now

    Featuring Criterion’s glorious restoration of Satyajit Ray’s Apu Trilogy; and two new films in their Ithaca premieres.

  • Animation Specials!

    Stepping out of the big studios to celebrate an amazing array of independent animators!

  • An Indonesian Tragedy

    Commemorating the 50th anniversary of the Indonesian Genocide, 4 free films with intros, Thur afternoons in September.

  • Cinema in Sage Chapel

    View the surreal animations of famed animator Lawrence Jordan in this beautiful setting! (free)

  • Contemporary
    World Cinema

    Five critically acclaimed films, four of them Ithaca premieres!

  • Doc Spots

    Six more critically acclaimed documentaries, including two with Academy-award nominated filmmaker Marshall Curry.

  • Cornell Alums
    Make Movies

    The creative work of Cornell alums can be found throughout the history of film and the industry surrounding it; includes visits by the filmmakers!

  • Halloween
    at Cornell Cinema

    Spooktacular fun! Treats for the little goblins who join the Ithakid costume parade, and vampires & costumes in the eve!

  • Sing-along
    Sound of Music

    With M.C. Bob Proehl. Pull out your Lederhosen, polish up the glockenspiel and brush up your yodeling! Costumes encouraged!

  • IthaKid Film Festival

    Animation, a live action animal doc, and Sing-along Sound of Music; cosponsored with Ithaca Youth Bureau.

  • Film Favorites

    Recent arthouse & Hollywood hits.
