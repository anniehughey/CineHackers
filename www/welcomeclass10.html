orientation week fall 2006

Cornell Cinema is considered one of the best campus film exhibition programs in the country, and we invite you to sample the wide variety of films we offer during Orientation Week. Throughout the academic year Cornell Cinema screens hundreds of recent and classic films in our 35mm Dolby Surround-Sound equipped theatres.

All screenings between August 20 - 24 will be held in Willard Straight Theatre. Screenings on Friday and Saturday, August 25 & 26, will be held in both Willard Straight Theatre and Uris Hall Auditorium. New students (grads and undergrads) get in for FREE from August 20-24!
